We get stuck in with local communities to cultivate lasting change in their neighbourhoods – whether it’s revitalising forgotten spaces, creating healthier environments or getting people excited about growing, foraging and eating healthy food. Our tree planting events are held in Autumn and Winter, as this gives the trees the best chance of survival. Trees for Cities are extremely grateful for the high level of volunteer enquiries we receive for planting days!
